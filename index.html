Mission Brief
Why The Academy Exists
"The First Fleet learned everything the hard way. The Second Fleet Academy exists so you don't have to."

Founded in the deep lanes between systems, RAM Space Industries grew from a small crew of contractors bound by one conviction: that the right team, given the right mission, has no ceiling.

Every hard lesson from those early years — every ambush, every failed cargo run, every callout that came too late — is now a training module in the Academy. What once cost ships and credits now costs a few hours in orientation.

When you graduate, you don't just join an org. You join a second fleet built on everything the first one learned.

Core Values
R

Reliability

If you sign up for an op, you show up. Your crew depends on you. The verse doesn't pause for no-shows.

A

New patches, new metas, new threats. RAM members learn fast and flex faster. Rigidity is a liability in space.

M

Mastery

Know your role. Whatever position you hold on a ship, be the best at it you can be. Expertise saves lives — and cargo.

Welcome, Cadet. You have taken the first step toward joining RAM Space Industries. Before you earn your division assignment and your crew rank, you need to understand who we are, how we operate, and what we expect of every person who flies under the RAMSORG tag.

Who We Are

RAM Space Industries was founded on a simple idea: that the right crew, given the right mission, has no ceiling. We are lawful, role-play friendly, and active across every major gameplay loop.

The RAM Doctrine

  • Reliability — If you sign up, you show up. Your crew plans around you.
  • Adaptability — The verse changes with every patch. Stay flexible.
  • Mastery — Know your role. Do it well.
Cadet note: Cadet is your rank while you complete the Academy. Once you graduate you become a Crew Member and receive your official division assignment.
02
Rank Structure & Progression
Unread
▾

RAMSORG uses a nine-rank system across three tiers. Understanding where you sit and how to move up is essential.

The Three Tiers

  • Command Tier — Admiral, Fleet Commander, Captain. Set strategy, run fleet ops, final authority.
  • Officer Tier — Division Head, Lieutenant, Specialist. Leadership and mentorship within each division.
  • Crew Tier — Senior Crew, Crew Member, Cadet. Where you start. The backbone of every op.

How You Advance

Rank is earned through Standing Points (SP) — awarded for attending events, completing ops, recruiting members, and teaching in the Academy.

Your current rank: Cadet. Next milestone: Crew Member — earned by completing all Academy stages and attending your first live op.

04
Events, Ops & How We Run Them
Unread
▾

RAMSORG runs a structured weekly event schedule. Know these before your first live op.

Weekly Rhythm

  • Monday — Open Contracts Night: Informal. No assigned roles. Great for your first session.
  • Wednesday — Division Ops: Structured. Each division runs its own focused activity.
  • Saturday — Fleet Op: Main event. Multi-ship, all divisions. Earns the most SP.
  • Monthly — Org Meeting: Voice meeting. Promotions, strategy, next month planned.

Fleet Op Flow

  • Announced 3+ days ahead in #fleet-ops-announcements.
  • Sign up by reacting with your division emoji or posting your role.
  • Captain assigns positions 24 hours before.
  • Everyone joins voice for a muster brief — objective, callsigns, rules of engagement.
  • After the op: after-action report. This is how SP are awarded.
Comms rule: In a live op, keep voice clear. Call out threats, statuses, positions — nothing else until debrief.
05
Standing Points & The Org Economy
Unread
▾

Standing Points (SP) track your contribution and progress toward rank.

How You Earn SP

  • Casual event attendance — +1 SP
  • Division Op completion — +2 SP
  • Fleet Op participation — +3 SP
  • MVP / Commendation — +2 SP bonus
  • Recruiting a new member — +3 SP
  • Teaching an Academy module — +4 SP
  • Org fund contributions — +1–5 SP at leadership discretion

Rank Thresholds

  • 5 SP — eligible for Crew Member
  • 20 SP — eligible for Senior Crew
  • 40 SP — eligible for Specialist
  • 60 SP — eligible for Lieutenant / Division Head

Code of Conduct & Discord
Unread
▾

RAMSORG runs on trust, respect, and Discord. These rules apply to every member from Cadet to Admiral.

Code of Conduct

  • Treat every member with respect. Zero tolerance for harassment.
  • No griefing org members or allies. Disputes go to a Division Head.
  • If you commit to an op, show up. Notify leadership in advance if you can't.
  • Follow your Captain's orders in an op. Save disagreements for debrief.
  • No unauthorized PvP — check rules of engagement before engaging.
